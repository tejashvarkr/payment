    At Ashraya Foundation, we go beyond just providing books and education—we strive to transform lives. Our mission is to empower underprivileged communities by addressing their most pressing needs. From distributing free books and offering quality education to providing food assistance that helps children stay in school

    We also organize skill development programs to equip individuals with tools for a brighter future and support families in overcoming challenges. Together, we aim to break the cycle of poverty and build a community where every child has the opportunity to learn, grow, and succeed..

    Our vision is to create a world where every child, regardless of their circumstances, has access to quality education, essential resources, and the opportunity to thrive. We aspire to empower communities by breaking the barriers of poverty and building a future rooted in equality, learning, and hope.

    Our mission is to bridge the gap between dreams and opportunities. Through books, mentorship, and community-driven programs, we aim to light up young minds and build a brighter future

    Services

    Our NGO is expanding services to better support the community and create a bigger impact

    Skill Development

    We offer free tailoring and beautician courses to empower women with valuable skills that enable them to become self-reliant and financially independent.

    Orphan Care

    Our organization provides shelter and essential support to orphans, ensuring they have a safe, nurturing environment. We focus on their holistic development, offering education, care, and emotional support to help them build a brighter future.

    Education Support

    We provide books, stationery, and free English coaching classes to children in government schools and Anganwadi centers. This initiative aims to bridge the educational gap and offer children opportunities to learn and grow.

    Cleanliness Drives

    To raise awareness about cleanliness, we actively participate in cleaning and painting government bus stops. These efforts not only beautify public spaces but also encourage citizens to maintain a clean environment.

    Health Initiatives

    We distribute health kits to government schools, Anganwadi centers, orphanages, slum children, and public service departments like the police and BBMP. This initiative aims to promote hygiene and safeguard the health of underprivileged communities.

    Arts & Sports

    We organize art and sports competitions for underprivileged children to foster creativity, teamwork, and self-expression. Additionally, we conduct motivational sessions for college students to inspire confidence and goal-oriented growth.

    Frequently Asked Questions

    How can I contribute to the Ashraya Foundation?

    You can support us through donations, volunteering, or by spreading the word about our mission. For donations, we accept both one-time and recurring contributions. Visit our Donate page for more details.

    What types of donations do you accept?

    We accept donations in the form of money, books, school supplies, and other educational resources. Your contributions help us provide free books, cover school fees, and support our educational programs.

    How do you ensure the donations reach the people in need?

    We maintain transparency in all our operations. Donations are directly used for our programs, including distributing books, paying school fees, and providing community support. We regularly update our supporters with reports and stories of impact.

    Can I sponsor a child’s education?

    Yes, you can! By sponsoring a child’s education, you help cover their school fees, books, and other educational resources.

    What impact does Ashraya Foundation have on the community?

    Since our inception, we’ve impacted thousands of children by providing educational support, distributing thousands of books, and funding school fees for those in need. Your support helps us create lasting change in the lives of underprivileged children and their families.
